North Head Sanctuary Foundation is working with Government agencies towards the establishment of Car-rang-gel Sanctuary on North Head at the gateway of Sydney Harbour - a flagship for Australia's environmental resolve and a celebration of our natural and cultural heritage.

Damaged Arch

February 2008

2007 in Review - leasing of School of Artillery site buildings - opening of Visitor Centre in the Gatehouse. Truck damages the Arch leading to North Head.

Arch reconstruction

April 2008

Work has begun on the damaged Arch. Flora and Fauna talk by Nick Skelton. New Secretary - Kathy Ridge. Database of plants begun.

Proposed Nursery area

June 2008

The shed and the concrete washdown bays will house the Education Room and Nursery. Business Plan for Nursery Operation.

Arch finished

July 2008

Mike Baird attended a protest meeting entitled Penguins not Police. Bushcare Training Course; Walking Coastal Sydney inaugural walk - North Head section.

Dignitaries at launch

August 2008

Walking Coastal Sydney launch. AGM and a walk to the site where the Nursery will be. Building Backyard Habitats talk.

ESBS

September 2008

North Fort Management Plan. Penguins not Police protest. Nature of Manly exhibition. Banksia Scrub to Wetland Walk.

Eastern Suburbs
Banksia Scrub

October 2008

Community Partnership Agreement signed between Sydney Harbour Federation Trust and North Head Sanctuary Foundation. Wild about Manly competition. Threatened Species Network Grant awarded to the Foundation.

ESBS

November-December 2008

Thanks to Sydney Harbour Federation Trust for donating furniture; Ocean Care Day; Seed collection has begun for the Nursery operation.
