Australian Botanical Cards

Whenever there is mention of a rare, threatened or endangered plant chances are, that like a bee to pollen, you will find a botanical artist. So, true to form, Julie Holcombe (formerly Julie Nettleton), award winning artist, has become involved in a most rewarding and worthwhile project with the North Head Sanctuary Foundation Inc at North Head.

Julie's role as a botanical artist is to record in paintings the amazing flowering plants that grow on North Head. The paintings are made into greeting cards which are a true Australian made product. They are printed here in Australia onto recycled, chlorine free paper. The perfect card to send to a special person or to use for that special occasion. These cards are blank inside. Each card has a description of the flowering plant on the back.

You can purchase these cards by mail order and also my visiting our Education room on the weekends.

Prices (in Australian dollars) are $5.50 each for the blank inside cards.

Postage within Australia and handling are included in the prices and there is a discount of 10% for members of the North Head Sanctuary Foundation Inc.
