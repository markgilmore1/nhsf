Membership of North Head Sanctuary Foundation

North Head Sanctuary Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation, registered with the Commonwealth Government as an environmental organisation and has been granted gift recipient tax exemption.

As with most community groups encouraging volunteer participation, we welcome new members joining our ranks. You can ...

* join as a member, attend meetings and receive copies of newsletters.

* join as a member and volunteer to join the committee or one of the working groups.

* join as a member and volunteer to work on site - in the nursery, caring for seedlings after they are planted out or preparing new areas for planting.

* join as a member and volunteer to greet visitors in Building 20 and answer their questions about the history, heritage or biodiversity of North Head.

* donate money for use in the sanctuary whether or not you are a member.

 

Fees are payable each year but are kept to a minimum needed to cover costs
i.e. Individual $20, family $30, concession $10
