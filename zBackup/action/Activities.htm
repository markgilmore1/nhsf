Activities related to North Head

Despite a slow start, spring is now on show at North Head.

Book on a guided wildflower walk (2-2.5 hours) on: Sun 7 Sept, 2pm, Sat 20 Sept 2pm, Wed 1 Oct 10am, or Monday 7 Oct (public holiday) 10am.

All are fairly easy track walks.

Bookings essential. To sign up: Call in at Bandicoot Heaven (Bldg 20 on North Fort Road at the former School of Artillery, between 10am and 4pm on a Saturday or Sunday) OR email twswombat505@gmail.com with your Name, email address, Contact phone number on walk day & number of places for the walk.

We'll be back in touch closer to your walk date with more details.
